What is Speaky-Spooky

Competitive Debate,
Elevated

Speaky-Spooky is a structured competitive debate platform where participants engage in formal rounds, follow established judging criteria, and compete across categories — from Parliamentary Debate to British Style, Lincoln-Douglas to Policy.

Unlike casual debate clubs, we operate with professional-grade infrastructure: certified adjudicators, transparent scoring rubrics, live results, and verifiable certificates. Your performance here means something.

The Process

From Registration
to Recognition

01

Register & Profile

Create your debater profile. Select your experience level, preferred formats, and availability for scheduling.

02

Get Scheduled

Admin matches and slots you into rounds. You'll see your upcoming debate slots, opponents, and motions on your dashboard.

03

Train & Prepare

Access curated training materials issued by the admin team — motion packs, argument frameworks, and video resources.

04

Debate & Score

Compete in your round. Results are posted to your dashboard, with detailed score breakdowns and adjudicator notes.

05

Climb the Leaderboard

Points accumulate. Watch your rank rise. Top performers earn certificates, medals, and invitations to championship rounds.
